PRODUCT FUNCTIONALITY PRODUCT PLANS
Role Based Dashboard
A collection of quick views uniquely designed to streamline user tasks.

Case Management
Track and manage case files, targets, informants, wire taps, undercover activities and the ability to restrict access for highly sensitive cases.

Fund and Asset Management
Track and manage taskforce fund distributions and receipts, undercover drug purchases, informant fees, miscellaneous expenditures and special operation seizures (drugs, guns, vehicles and money).

Team Management
Track and manage enforcement teams, leaders, members and their system access.

Event Notification
Provides email and text messaging notifications to streamline day to day taskforce activities.

Data Analysis
Provides data analysis for fund management as well as system information.

System Security
Provides secure access through user authentication and authorization, with support for communication and data encryption.
